## How to Buy Tickets

### Step 1: Connect Wallet

1. Visit the Tradmatrix app
2. Click "Select Wallet" in top right
3. Choose Phantom
4. Approve connection

### Step 2: Browse Assets

View all available assets on the homepage:
- See asset name and images
- Check tickets available
- View ticket cost
- Monitor progress bar

### Step 3: Select Asset

Click on any asset card to see:
- Full asset details
- Current tickets sold / total tickets
- List of ticket buyers
- Win probability calculator

### Step 4: Purchase Tickets

1. Click "Buy Ticket" button
2. Select quantity (more tickets = higher odds)
3. Review:
   - Total cost in USDC
   - Your win probability
   - Expected returns
4. Click "Buy Tickets"
5. Approve transaction in Phantom

<Note>
  First-time buyers: Your user account will be auto-initialized (costs a small amount of SOL)
</Note>

### Step 5: Track Your Tickets

- View your tickets in "My Tickets" section
- Monitor asset progress
- Check if you won after sellout

## Understanding Probability

Your win chance is simple math:

```
Probability = (Your Tickets) / (Total Tickets Remaining)
```

### Examples

Asset with 5,000 tickets:

| Your Tickets | Investment | Win Probability |
|--------------|------------|-----------------|
| 1 | $100 | 0.02% |
| 10 | $1,000 | 0.20% |
| 50 | $5,000 | 1.00% |
| 100 | $10,000 | 2.00% |

<Tip>
  **Strategy**: Spread investments across multiple assets for diversification, or concentrate on one for higher individual odds.
</Tip>

## Safety Tips

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How much does it cost to participate?">
    - **Ticket price**: Set by asset owner (e.g., $100)
    - **Transaction fee**: ~$0.00001 SOL (negligible)
    - **No platform fee** for buyers!
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I buy multiple tickets?">
    Yes! Buy as many as you want to increase your odds.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="How is the winner selected?">
    Using Entropy's provably fair algorithm - cryptographically secure, completely random, impossible to predict or manipulate.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I don't win?">
    Your ticket cost goes to the asset owner (minus 1.5% platform fee). You took a chance at winning a high-value asset for a fraction of its cost.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I sell my ticket?">
    Currently tickets are non-transferable. Secondary marketplace coming in Phase 3.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if asset doesn't sell out?">
    Currently there's no refund mechanism. Only participate in assets you believe will sell out. Future versions may add refund logic.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
